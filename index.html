Top three activities to do in Shreveport

Visit a local park

There are many wonderful parks all around Shreveport where you can bring your dog or have a picnic with friends.

Gamble at the Casinos

All along the Red River, there are multiple casinos to visit.

Semi-pro Soccer Team

With a strong soccer community, the Shreveport Rafters' games are always fun at Independence Stadium.
